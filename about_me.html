My Italian Getaway

I had the pleasure of traveling to Italy in May of 2023 with both of my parents and my two sisters. We traveled to Rome, Florence, and Praino. I will be attending a wedding in Italy in May of 2027 and I can't wait to go back!

My Favorite Restaurant

Go to Pappare Boston

My favorite restaurant is Pappare in Boston's North End. The neighborhood has a great atmosphere and my favorite dish is their penne alla vodka with a crispy chicken cutlet.

My Favorite Food

My favorite food is pizza, especially when it's from New York. I like making homemade pizza, as well.
